Travel to Chichen Itza

New wonder of the world

Chichen Itza is one of the 7 new wonders of the world according to UNESCO.

Localization

It is located in Yucatan, Mexico in the middle between Merida and Cancun. To arrive you can take a 2 hours and a half touristic bus from Cancun.

History

It was built in the IX century to adore Chaac (the rain god) by the Mayan civilization and it served as an important religious and cultural center of the zone.

"El Castillo" pyramid

Is the pyramid of the zone, rising to 24 meters. This structure has 91 stairs on the four cardinal directions, combining 365 steps, like the solar year.

Astronomy

The zone has an observatory called "El Caracol" that has evidence of the advanced knowledge the Mayan's had in astronomy and math.

Ball game (tlachtli)

It has a court of the game that was used as a sacred ritual and had the objective to pass a ball into the rings, using the hips.
